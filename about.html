Critter Tracker



This application displays the critters available to your character in their respective hemisphere based on your device's clock. You can also set a custom clock if you're time travelling ;)

App is non-profit, open source, and free to use-
All Data Sourced from Animal Crossing Fandom Wiki
Note from dev: -- I got sick of going over the tables on the wikis and just wanted something to display critters in real time. So yea. Here ya go. The site is hosted on github.io for the time being.
